We support fellowships and organisations working for the environment, with a focus on afforestation, ecological regeneration and livelihoods.

Who are we?

Rainmatter Foundation is an initiative by the team behind Zerodha, India's largest stock broking firm. We understand that technology alone will not solve for climate change. So, we have committed to using the resources available to us to support efforts in the areas of environmental conservation & rejuvenation and sustainable livelihoods. Learn more.

Funding

We fund fellowships and organisations working to restore the environment through direct action, research, climate advocacy and policy. We do this through investments, grants and fellowships.
